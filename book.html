BOOKS IN THE

LIBRARY

We look forward to welcoming publishers and authors to the 2024 Brooklyn Book Festival. Our outdoor Literary Marketplace welcomes more than 200 publishers each year and thousands of visitors stroll through to enjoy the incredible array of books, publishers and literary organizations participating in the Festival. Children’s Day will be September 28th and Festival Day will be September 29th.

Registration information and fees will be available on the website starting April 1st, 2024

Please contact jallred@brooklynbookfestival.org if you have questions regarding the Festival.

Alert: all Marketplace registrations take place through the Brooklyn Book Festival only. If you are approached by any other entity or person, they do not represent the Festival.

Reach out for more info

To be placed on the mailing list to receive registration and pricing information, please contact Jerry Allred at jallred@brooklynbookfestival.org

We are now accepting vendors for our wait list only.

The Brooklyn Book Festival is New York City’s largest free literary Festival and welcomes over 30,000 people each year!
