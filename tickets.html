The Brooklyn Book Festival is NYC’s book festival with its heart and home in Brooklyn.

We are looking forward to September when we present nine days of literary events, including literary Bookend events throughout the city, our flagship Festival Day & Literary Marketplace in Downtown Brooklyn, and our popular Children’s Day in Brooklyn Commons.

Buying tickets in advance online will speed up the process at the ticket hut, so you can enjoy a stress-free time in the books festival!

The details:

September 22 2024 12pm - 5pm Virtual Festival Day September 22, 2024 — September 30, 2024 Bookends

The mission of the Brooklyn Book Festival (BKBF) is to celebrate published literature and nurture a literary cultural community through programming that cultivates and connects readers of diverse ages and backgrounds with local, national and international authors, publishers and booksellers.

Tickets are non-refundable or transferable.

To this end the Brooklyn Book Festival develops original programming that is hip, smart, diverse, and inclusive and presents free, literary, cultural events at the Brooklyn Book Festival Day & Literary Marketplace, Virtual Festival Day, and the family friendly Children’s Day.

Additionally, BKBF presents Bookend events in venues across NYC and on-line, which are overwhelmingly presented free to the public.
